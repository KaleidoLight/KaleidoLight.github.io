Kaleidolight: The World through my lens

Hi there,

My name is Alberto Presti and I am a student of Biomedical Engineering. When I'm not studying or preparing for exams, you'll likely find me with my camera in hand. Recently, I have developed a passion for street photography and have created a project called Kaleidolight to showcase my work.

Kaleidolight is a project born in 2018 with the aim of capturing the best of my photographic experiences and travels, with a particular focus on street photography. What fascinates me most about this art form is the uncertainty of how a great photo will turn out, yet at the same time the impulse to capture the moment in front of me. Whether it's people on a bus, playing with shadows, or reflecting in shop windows, the possibilities to create something interesting are endless.
